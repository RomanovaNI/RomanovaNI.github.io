The place that will never let you indifferent

Once upon a time I arrived at this place and my heart started to beat different. I opened for myself that the life can be much more than routine. Maybe routine but routine with taste, colours and every single day happiness. This quite small state has everything what your soul wishes. I invite you to discover this region with me.

Nature

California is the region with variety of nature’s miracles: my favourite spot is PSSS. I call so Pacific Ocean, Sand, Sun and Sky. Once you look up you ll be in love with amazing mix of blue sky and white clouds, looks like Monet was here and painted it. The strong energy of Pacific captures your heart. Sun presents you every day happy mood and smile. Once you meet here sunset, you ll never forget it’s colourfulness. California suggests you also mountains, worldwide famous parks and much breathtaking views.
